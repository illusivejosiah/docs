Funds are used to purchase proxies and other services on Illusory platform. Adding funds also allows you to use Auto-Renew feature, which automatically renews your proxies when they expire. Visit [Illusory App](https://app.illusory.io/funds) to add funds to your account.

<Check>
  Illusory uses Stripe and Radom to process payments. We do not store any of your payment information on our servers.
</Check>
