title: 'Android WiFi'
description: 'Using Illusory with Android.'
---

<Steps>
    <Step title="Find WiFi Network" stepNumber="1">
        Go to the `Settings` app and select Wifi & Internet. Find your Wi-Fi network. If you’re currently connected to
        it, it should be the topmost option.

        <Frame>
            <img src="/images/connect/android/wifi/how-to-set-up-proxy-on-android-1.png"/>
        </Frame>

    </Step>
    <Step title="Modify Network" stepNumber="2">
        Select the Gear icon on the right side and tap `Modify`. This will open a preferences popup. If you can’t see
        the Gear icon, touch and hold the network name and tap Modify Network.

        <Frame>
            <img src="/images/connect/android/wifi/how-to-set-up-proxy-on-android-2.png"/>
        </Frame>

    </Step>
    <Step title="Select Manual" stepNumber="3">
        Within the proxy modification menu, select `Manual`. This means that you’ll enter your proxy server’s settings.

        <Frame>
            <img src="/images/connect/android/wifi/how-to-set-up-proxy-on-android-3.png"/>
        </Frame>

    </Step>
    <Step title="Select Manual" stepNumber={5}>
        Within the proxy modification menu, select `Manual`. This means that you’ll enter your proxy server’s settings.

        <Frame>
            <img src="/images/connect/android/wifi/how-to-set-up-proxy-on-android-4.png"/>
        </Frame>

    </Step>
    <Step title="Test">
        Go to [What Is My IP Address](https://whatismyipaddress.com/) to see if your IP address and location have
        changed.
    </Step>
</Steps>
