---
title: 'Android WiFi'
description: 'Using Illusory with Android.'
---

<Steps>
    <Step title="Find WiFi Network">
        Go to the `Settings` app and select Wifi & Internet. Find your Wi-Fi network. If you’re currently connected to it,
        it should be the topmost option.

        <Frame>
            <img src="/images/connect/android/how-to-set-up-proxy-on-android-mobile-data-1.png" />
        </Frame>


    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Proxy Details">
        Scroll to HTTP Proxy and tap `Configure Proxy`.

        Select `Manual`.

        Tap `Server` and enter the proxy server address.

        <Note>
            If you’ve authenticated by whitelisting your IP address, you don’t need to enter the credentials. We
            recommend using whitelist IP for Android and iOS connections for ease of use.
        </Note>

        Tap `Save` to enable the setting.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Test">
        Go to [What Is My IP Address](https://whatismyipaddress.com/) to see if your IP address and location have
        changed.
    </Step>
</Steps>
